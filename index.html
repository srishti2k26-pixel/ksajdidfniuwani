About The Fest

Srishti-2k26 is the annual cultural festival of our institution I.Ag.Sc BHU, bringing together talent from across the institute for four days of non-stop entertainment, competition, and celebration.

With over 30 events spanning music, dance, drama, art, literature, and more, Srishti promises to be an unforgettable experience. Our theme this year is "Convergence of Cultures" celebrating the diversity that makes our world beautiful.

Join us from Jan 31st-3rd Feb , 2026 at the Auditorium for an experience you'll cherish forever.
