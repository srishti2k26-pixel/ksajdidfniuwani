About The Fest

Srishti-2k26 is the premier annual cultural festival of I.Ag.Sc BHU, bringing together the brightest talents from across the institute for four exhilarating days of entertainment, competition, and celebration.

With over 30 events spanning music, dance, drama, art, literature, and more, Srishti promises to be an unforgettable experience. Our theme this year is "Convergence of Cultures" ‚Äì a celebration of the beautiful diversity that enriches our world.
